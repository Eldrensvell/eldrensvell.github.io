Echoes in the Dark

A liminal, exploration-horror game where the reality you don't see can shift

Game Overview

Echoes in the Dark is a liminal exploration-horror game where you have to explore a version of your house that seems... off.

As reality shifts around you, solve puzzles and avoid any potential threats to get out alive.

  • Uncover how the reality around you behaves and shifts when your attention decrease
  • Explore various areas with growing threats
  • Find the exit to get back to reality alive

Development Status

The game is currently in active development. Follow us for updates, behind-the-scenes content, and maybe early access opportunities in the future!
